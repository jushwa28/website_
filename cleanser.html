Cleanser

Luxe Organix Power Glow Creamy Whip Cleanser cleanses the face without the stripping feeling.
Developed with Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate and Vitamin C to reduce signs of aging.
It promotes a brighter complexion by reducing impurities and increasing collagen production, revealing a healthier glow.

How To Use

Squeeze a pea-sized amount onto hands, gently massage into a damp face in circular motions, avoiding the eye area. Rinse thoroughly with water.

Ingredients

Water, Glycerin, Palmitic Acid, Stearic Acid, Myristic Acid, Potassium Hydroxide, Lauric Acid, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Sorbitan Olivate, Cocamide MEA, Allantoin, Butylene Glycol, Sodium Chloride, Disodium EDTA, Capric Acid, Glyceryl Stearate, PEG-100 Stearate, PEG-20M, Silica BHT, Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate, Ascorbic Acid, Caprylyl Glycol, Hydroxyacetophenone, Pentylene Glycol, 1,2-Hexanediol, Fragrance.

Caution

13 years old and above may use this product

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Conduct a skin test first. Stop using and consult a doctor if skin becomes red,
swollen and itchy during use. Keep out of reach of children. Store at room temperature
