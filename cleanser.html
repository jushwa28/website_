Cleanser

Luxe Organix Power Glow Creamy Whip Cleanser cleanses face without the stripping feeling.
Developed with Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate and Vitamin C to reduce signs of aging.
It promotes a brighter complexion by reducing impurities and increasing collagen production, revealing a healthier glow.


How To Use

Squeeze a pea sized amount onto hands, gently massage into damp face in circular motions, avoiding the eye area. Rinse thoroughly with water.
