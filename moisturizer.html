Moisturizer

Luxe Organix Power Glow Gel Cream is a lightweight moisturizer packed with ingredients that help nourish and hydrate the skin. Its lightweight consistency makes it ideal for day and night use. With Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate and Vitamin C that helps give the skin a healthy glow while minimizing pores.

How To Use

After cleansing, toning and water-based serum, apply a proper amount and evenly massage on the skin until completely absorbed.

Ingredients

Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate, Water, Butylene Glycol, Cyclopentasiloxane, Glycerin, Polysorbate 20, Octyldodecanol, Carbomer, Arginine,
Coceth-7, Ascorbic Acid, PPG-1-PEG-9 Lauryl Glycol Ether, Caprylyl Glycol, Dimethicone/Vinyl Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Pentylene Glycol,
Sodium Polyacrylate, Olea Europaea (Olive) Leaf Extract, Ascorbyl Glucoside, Zinc PCA, PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor oil, Dimethiconol,
Hydroxyacetophenone, Disodium EDTA, 1,2-Hexanediol, Fragrance

Caution

13 years old and above may use this product

Keep out of reach of children. Avoid contact with the eyes. If contact occurs rinse immediately with water.
If any irritation occurs dicontinue use and consult a doctor if necessary.
