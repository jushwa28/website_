Moisturizer

Luxe Organix Power Glow Gel Cream is a lightweight moisturizer packed with ingredients that help nourish and hydrate the skin. Its lightweight consistency makes it ideal for day and night use. With Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate and Vitamin C that helps give the skin a healthy glow while minimizing pores.


How To Use

After cleansing, toning and water-based serum, apply a proper amount and evenly massage on the skin until completely absorbed.
