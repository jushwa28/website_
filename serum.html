Serum

Luxe Organix Mirale Repair Serum is infused with Vitamin E Licorice Extract Enriched with Niacinamide and Licorice Extracts
that help fade dark spots and pigmentation caused by acne while increases collagen production to help improve skin's barrier. With powerful benefits of
Vitamin E to fight free radicals, skin becomes healthier with every use.



How To Use

Apply a thin layer to clean face after toner and before heavier creams. Gently tap your face until product has been
fully absorbed. Always wear sunscreen when using this product during day time as it increases sun sensitivity.
