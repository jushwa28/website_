Toner

Luxe Organix Miracle Solutions Exfoliating Toner is an all-in-one gentle toner and exfoliator that helps balance the PH levels of your skin. AHA works to gently exfoliate dead skin cells while BHAs help unclog pores, leaving your complexion smoother, clearer and more even-toned. This product contains tea tree oil known for its anti-inflammatory power which is also suitable for acne-prone skin.

How To Use

Soak a cotton pad with an adequate amount of toner. Gently apply to face, avoiding the eye and mouth area. Let toner remain on face until fully absorbed. To attain its full benefits, ensure to have adequate sunscreen protection while using this product.

Ingredients

Water, Alcohol Denat, 1,2-Hexanediol, Niacinamide, Arginine, Glycolic Acid, PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, Butylene Glycol, Disodium EDTA, Salicylic Acid, Melaleuca Alternifolia (Tea Tree) Leaf Oil, Gluconic Acid, Mandelic Acid, Lactobionic Acid, Sodium Hyroxide
